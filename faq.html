Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Information

Where is Kampung Wai located?

Kampung Wai is located in Perlis, the northernmost state of Malaysia, near the Thailand border.

What is the best time to visit Kampung Wai?

The best time to visit is during the dry season, from November to February, when the weather is cooler and there are fewer rain showers.

About Kampung Wai

What is the history of Kampung Wai?

Kampung Wai has a rich history that dates back several centuries. It is known for its cultural heritage and historical landmarks.

What wildlife can be found in Kampung Wai?

The area is home to various bird species, butterflies, and local fauna, making it a great spot for nature enthusiasts.

Attractions and Activities

What are the main attractions in Kampung Wai?

Some attractions include historical sites, traditional Malay houses, local markets, and beautiful natural landscapes.

Are there any outdoor activities available?

Yes, visitors can enjoy activities such as hiking and exploring the local flora and fauna.

Travel and Accommodation

How can I get to Kampung Wai?

Kampung Wai can be accessed by road from major cities in Malaysia. The nearest airport is in Alor Setar, Kedah.

What accommodation options are available in Kampung Wai?

There are several guesthouses and homestays offering comfortable lodging.

Visitor Information

Is Kampung Wai family-friendly?

Yes, Kampung Wai is a family-friendly destination with activities for all ages.

Are there any local guides available?

Yes, local guides are available to help you explore the village.
